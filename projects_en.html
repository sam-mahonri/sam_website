Description
“Nightmare Fable” will be a horror/thriller game with a graphic style based on the early 3D like on the games of PS1 although with some modern details, the game has as it main objective bringing an old game air and work as tribute for an era and a specific style. This game is being developed with Godot Game Engine 4.X and it’s already being prepared for all the platforms including mobile dispositives.
Development stage
The game has passed the stage of prototypes and tests, the first pilot versions are already in progress.
Synopsis
You find yourself following the Sunset’s life, a charismatic bat and by your side is your friend Rover, an astute and conscious dog, they live peaceful moments in a park in the quiet town. Everything changes when a mysterious light takes them deep inside the forest and Rover is captured, leaving Sunset devastated and surrounded by loneliness. Decided to find his loyal friend, Sunset enters a journey into the night passing by three uncharted kingdoms. There, he came across with a gigantic three horned god and characters which he called alienated, that have such uncanny motivations as the light that brought them. Facing the darkness and the dangers of the night, Sunset finds the true force of friendship while revealing secrets on the dark nooks. This research becomes a self discovery journey, where Sunset finds courage and wiseness in his fight against the challenges that surround him. An engaging and touching story about friendship and the resilience against loneliness, Sunset confronts the obstacles and defiances with braveness, proving that true bonds can illuminate even the darkest of the nights.

Description
The main goal of Licse You is to offer a collaborative AI in which students and teachers can contribute with your knowledge. Its main use is in the academic area, which any school or university who’s going to use it can personalize it to respond to questions related with the specific institution.
